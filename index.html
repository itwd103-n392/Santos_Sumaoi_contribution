About the | |BROTHERHOOD

We (Anbu Black Ops) are recruited from our village's standard shinobi forces, hand-picked by our Kage for their individual capabilities and special skills. Age, background, gender, or previous rank bear no significance in this decision. While on the job, We wear masks and use code names to conceal our identity, leaving only our Kage informed.

What we Do

We typically work in teams, formed on the mission's requirements. There are apparently no true ranks within the Anbu; team leadership and hierarchy seem to be based on merit and experience. The leaders of the teams are called squad leaders (分隊長, Buntaichō), a position held in high regard.
